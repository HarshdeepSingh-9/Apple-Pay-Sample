Light Mode Demo

Light mode is a display setting or design choice in software applications, websites, or operating systems where the overall color scheme is light or bright. It is often used as an alternative to dark mode, which features darker colors, especially for background elements. The choice between light mode and dark mode is typically a matter of personal preference, and many applications and websites provide users with the option to switch between the two modes.

Dark Mode Demo

Dark mode is a user interface (UI) design trend and feature that provides an alternative color scheme to the traditional light mode. In dark mode, the background colors of the interface, as well as the colors of text and other elements, are inverted to darker shades. This design choice is intended to reduce eye strain, improve visibility in low-light environments, and potentially save energy on devices with certain types of displays.
